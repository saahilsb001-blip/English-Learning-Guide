1. Helping Verbs (Trishul)

These 3 words dictate the time of action:

Hindi Time English
Tha / The Past Was, Were, Had, Did
Hai / Hoon Present Is, Am, Are, Has, Have
Hoga / Hogi Future Will, Will be

Always identify the helping verb first!

2. Verb Forms (V1 to V5)

Form Example
V1 (Base) Go, Play, Eat
V2 (Past) Went, Played, Ate
V3 (Perfect) Gone, Played, Eaten
V4 (-ing) Going, Playing
V5 (-s/-es) Goes, Plays, Eats
